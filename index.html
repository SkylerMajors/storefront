About Us

We offer pure and natural Indiana Honey. David has been a hobby beekeeper for 45 years learning beekeeping from his grandfather. Upon retirement from his factory job, the hobby became a business.

Indiana beekeepers offering local honey at various farmers markets and festivals. Nationwide shipping is also available.

Products while not certified organic, we take pride to offer you a honey that is naturally delicious. Our liquid extracted honey is available in both glass and plastic jars. We also offer several handmade whipped honey varieties, honeycomb when available as well as Apple Butter sweetened only with honey and Honey Peanut Butter.













Raw Honey

Our Raw Honey comes in a variety of sizes: 2oz, 8oz, 12oz bears, 12oz Trail Rations, 1lb, 2lbs, 3lbs, 4lbs

Whipped Honey

Our Whipped Honey is made using our raw honey and comes in two sizes: 11oz and 5oz. They come in kdjf flavors: regular, blueberry, blackberry, cinnamon, peach, strawberry, and jalepeno.

Chunk Honey

Our Chunk Honey uses small rectangles of our comb honey submerged in our Raw Honey. It comes in three sizes: 1/2pint, 1pint, and 2lbs.

Honey Sticks

Honey sticks are your own personal serving of honey in a straw. They come in a large selection of flavors: pure honey, blueberry, strawberry, rootbeer, chocolate, cinnamon, pina calada, orange, grape, watermelon, peach, and many more!

Bee's Wax

We sell a variety of Bee's wax candles, ordaments, and 1oz or 1lb blocks.

Comb honey

Our freshly cut Comb Honey comes in one pound square boxes.
